<br> #### Week 3: Kinetic Sculpture Objectives: Create a kinetic sculpture. Include circuitry to move your sculpture. Use Ohm's law to calculate current through the circuit. The theme of my week 3 project is “Matching to the tone”. When I was younger, I was in a marching band. I used to march and play music for hours in parades and had so much fun doing it. I thought it would be cool to make something that reminds me of those days.
